Privacy Policy — Contractors Kitchen

Last updated: 06/12/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Contractors Kitchen (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, protects, and shares your information when you use our finance and accountancy application (“the App”). By using Contractors Kitchen, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to support financial calculations, reporting, and communication with clients.

1.1 Personal Information

  • Name
  • NI Number
  • Address
  • Bank details (account number and sort code)
  • UTR Number
  • Company number
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Business details (company name, registration number, industry)
  • Client contact information

1.2 Financial and Accounting Data

  • Transaction records
  • Invoices, receipts, and bills
  • Tax-related documents
  • Uploaded accounting or financial files

1.3 Usage Data

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • IP address
  • Log files, error logs, and diagnostic data
  • App interaction data (pages viewed, features used)

1.4 Voluntary Information

Any data you provide through forms, surveys, or customer support channels.

1.5 Financial Information We Collect

We collect the following bank details from you:

  • Account Number
  • Sort Code

Why We Collect This Information

We only use this information for record-keeping and reference purposes. It is not used for payments, transfers, or any automated banking actions.

How Your Information Is Stored

Your bank details are stored securely inside our system. They are not visible to the public and can only be accessed by authorised team members who need the information for support or verification.

How We Protect Your Information

We use security measures such as encryption, restricted access, and secure data storage protocols to keep your bank details safe.

Who We Share Your Information With

We do not share your bank details with any third parties unless required by law.

Your Rights (Bank Details)

You can:

  • Ask to see what bank information we hold
  • Ask us to correct it
  • Ask us to delete it

To do this, you can contact us at any time using the details on our Support page.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide financial tools, calculations, and accounting features
  • Prepare financial reports, summaries, and other documentation
  • Facilitate communication between you and your clients
  • Improve our services and app performance
  • Respond to support requests
  • Meet legal, tax, and regulatory obligations under UK law

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your information based on:

  • Providing and delivering the services you request
  • Improving the App and ensuring secure functionality
  • Meeting legal and regulatory requirements
  • Your consent for optional features or communications

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We only share your information in these cases:

4.1 Service Providers

Trusted third parties may process data on our behalf for:

  • Hosting and cloud services
  • Data processing and backup
  • Customer support tools

4.2 Legal Obligations

We may disclose information when required to:

  • Comply with UK law or court orders
  • Assist law enforcement
  • Protect our rights, safety, or users

4.3 With Your Permission

We may share financial reports or documents with your clients when instructed by you.

5. Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to secure your data. While we strive to protect your information, no electronic storage method is completely secure.

6. Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as necessary for:

  • Providing services
  • Meeting legal and tax requirements
  • Resolving disputes

You may request deletion of your data unless we are required to retain it by law.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request corrections or updates
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict or object to certain processing
  • Withdraw consent for optional features or communications

8. Communication Features

The App may send messages, notifications, or reports to help you manage financial interactions. All communication data is stored securely and used strictly for accountancy-related services.

9. Third-Party Links

The App may include links to external websites or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content.

10. Children’s Privacy

Contractors Kitchen is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use of the App indicates acceptance of any changes.
